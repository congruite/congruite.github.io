COMPLIANCE & RISK

KYC
In today's high-tech world, cyber criminals are more sophisticated than ever before. Identity thieves work hand-in-hand with financial terrorist to infiltrate your financial systems right through the front door. This creates a critical need for new and intelligent ways to verify the identity of your customers.

Yantra's KYC service enables your organization to better comply with the US Patriot Act and Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) requirements through the collection and analysis of multiple customer data points during the sign-up process. This includes;

OFAC

Checks all customer names against FINCEN's OFAC List and matches as well as partial matches are identified.

Address Verification

Match customer provided information with the information maintained at selected global databases. The KYC decision making model can be customized to produce granular assessments including PASS, FAIL and further screening with Out of Wallet questions.

Location Detection

Compare the address provided by your prospective customer during the application process with the current location of their computer's IP address and mobile number for a triangulated review and verification.

REAL-TIME TRANSACTION RISK MANAGEMENT

With increasing regulatory emphasis on Anti-Money Laundering monitoring, the demand for fast processing of payments, real-time risk-scoring and the development of new risk detection models has become critical. Financial criminals continually improve their ability to infiltrate existing banking systems — Yantra's Compliance & Risk Management System (CRMS) is the first payments specific risk-scoring solution which leverages the latest technologies and complex mathematical algorithms to combat this threat.

CRMS is built on a big-data technology stack which delivers scalable and fast data processing performance. Current performance testing across millions of financial transactions and OFAC queries has shown impressive results. CRMS is able to return a search result within 20 milliseconds to the end-user.

The system is offered to banks and commercial clients as a hosted solution. Enterprise level clients have the option for an in-house installation license. Regardless of the commercial model or client size, CRMS delivers a robust list of features which includes:

  • Simple integration and implementation.
  • APIs for real-time integration into existing core
  • processing systems
  • Fast search to find transactions
  • Configurable risk scoring system
  • Configurable rules and limits tracking
  • Risk Scoring across all historical transactions
  • Risk Scoring across all types of transactions
  • Case Management Tool for further analysis and resolution
  • Configurable threshold based alerting system
  • Role-Based access
  • Comprehensive Reporting Tool
